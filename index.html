Our donation will serve food packages to families. We will receive essential items/month. Send Family Food Kits that include nutritious meals like oatmeal, potato soup, pasta, and a bean and rice casserole.

Shelter For A family

Our Donation does accept in-kind donations of items our families can use either in the shelter program or as them move into transitional and permanent housing.
